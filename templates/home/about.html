PrivLink

About

PrivLink is an anonymous and encrypted URL shortener which promises no ads and no IP logs. All links are valid only for 12h and the entire project is open-source and does not have JS.

PrivLink uses AES-256 encryption to encrypt your link. Every randomly shortened link comes with two parts - the identifier and a password. PrivLink only stores the identifier, while the password which is used to encrypt the link, it is never stored in the database.

However, when you use your own custom name without a password, PrivLink is forced to generate a random password and store it in the database for decrypting it. Therefore, it is highly recommended that you use a password for all your links.

While you're here, feel free to check out another project to share one-time private messages.
